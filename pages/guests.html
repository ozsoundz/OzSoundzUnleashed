Oz Soundz Unleashed proudly features a diverse lineup of voices from across the Australian music and audio industry. As the official independent podcast of The Oz Soundz Group, the show brings together independent artists, producers, audio engineers, live sound technicians, venue operators, music educators, and industry professionals who are shaping the sound of Australia.

Our guests range from emerging indie talent building careers from home studios to seasoned professionals working in live production, recording, touring, and music business development. Every conversation is designed to provide authentic insight, practical knowledge, and real-world experience that benefits creators at every level.

Guest Focus Areas

▸Artist journeys and creative processes
▸DIY home recording and production workflows
▸Live sound and event production
▸Music business strategy and sustainability
▸Industry challenges and growth opportunities
