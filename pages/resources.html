Digital Distribution

Get Your Music Out There

Platforms for distributing your music to streaming services worldwide.

Distribution

DistroKid

Unlimited uploads for a flat annual fee. Fast distribution to Spotify, Apple Music, and 150+ platforms. Popular with indie artists globally.

 Distribution

TuneCore

Per-release pricing with 100% royalty retention. Strong in sync licensing and YouTube monetisation for Australian artists.

 Distribution

CD Baby

One-time fee per release. Includes physical distribution, sync licensing, and publishing administration services.

 Distribution

Amuse

Free distribution tier available. Used by many bedroom producers and emerging artists for zero-cost releases.

 🇦🇺 Australian

LocalSounds

Australian platform dedicated to local music discovery. Feature your music where Aussie fans are actively looking for local talent.

 🇦🇺 Australian

Music Australia

The peak body for the Australian music industry. Resources, advocacy, funding guidance, and industry connections.
Home Studio

Build Your Recording Setup

DAWs, plugins, and tools for home recording — from free to pro.

DAW

Ableton Live

Industry standard for electronic music production, live performance, and loop-based composition.

 DAW

Logic Pro

Mac-native DAW with incredible built-in instruments and plugins. A one-time purchase that punches well above its price.

 DAW · Budget

REAPER

Professional DAW at a fraction of the cost. Highly customisable, low on resources, massive community. Perfect for indie budgets.

 Free

Audacity

Free, open-source audio editor. Great for podcast recording, audio cleanup, and simple multitrack editing.

 Samples

Splice

Royalty-free sample subscription and plugin rental. Essential for bedroom producers building their sound library.

Music Business

Build a Sustainable Career

Rights, royalties, funding, and industry bodies every Australian musician should know.

🇦🇺 Rights

APRA AMCOS

Australia's performing rights organisation. Register your songs, collect royalties from radio, live performance, streaming, and more.

 🇦🇺 Archive

Australia Music Vault

Australia's music museum and archive. A reminder of the rich history you're building on.

 🇦🇺 Funding

Australia Council

Federal arts funding body. Grants available for recording projects, touring, career development, and more for Australian artists.

 Direct Sales

Bandcamp

Sell music and merch directly to fans. Artist-friendly revenue split. Still one of the best platforms for supporting indie music.

 Membership

Patreon

Build a subscriber base and offer exclusive content to your most dedicated fans. Sustainable recurring income for creators.

 Licensing

Songtradr

Music licensing marketplace. Get your music placed in film, TV, ads, and games — an underutilised revenue stream for indie artists.
