Who We Are

Oz Soundz Unleashed

Oz Soundz Unleashed is the official independent podcast of The Oz Soundz Group — created to spotlight Australia's thriving music community, champion independent artists, and explore the evolving world of audio production.

This isn't just another music podcast. This is where artists, engineers, producers, venue owners, and industry insiders come together to talk real stories, real gear, and real growth.

We focus on the people behind the sound — the creators building careers from bedrooms, garages, rehearsal spaces, and stages across the country.

Australia's independent music community is resilient, innovative, and constantly evolving. Oz Soundz Unleashed exists to amplify those voices — from Sydney rehearsal rooms to regional festival stages, from bedroom producers to touring indie bands.

As part of The Oz Soundz Group, the podcast operates with editorial independence while aligning with a bigger mission: strengthening Australia's music ecosystem through conversation, education, and community connection.
