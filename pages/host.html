Biography

Jason "Plummo" Plumridge

Jason "Plummo" Plumridge is the host of Oz Soundz Unleashed, the independent Australian music and audio podcast powered by The Oz Soundz Group.

With a deep passion for Australian independent music and the technical side of sound production, Plummo brings authentic industry insight, practical knowledge, and real-world experience to every episode. Known for his straight-talking style and genuine support of grassroots artists, he bridges the gap between musicians, producers, audio engineers, and live sound professionals.

Jason's background spans music, audio equipment, production knowledge, and industry networking — giving him a well-rounded perspective on both the creative and business sides of the Australian music scene. His focus is on empowering independent artists, demystifying home studio setups, and highlighting the opportunities within Australia's evolving music economy.

"Proudly Australian. Passionate about sound. Dedicated to supporting those who create it."

Through Oz Soundz Unleashed, Plummo champions emerging talent, explores DIY recording culture, and fosters conversations that strengthen Australia's independent music community.
