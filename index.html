About The Show

Built From
The Underground

Oz Soundz Unleashed is the podcast that refuses to play it safe. We go beyond the stage and into the real lives of the artists, producers, and industry veterans who've dedicated everything to the music they love.

No corporate filters. No PR spin. Just honest, raw conversations about the grind, the passion, the failures, and the breakthroughs that define the independent Australian music scene.

Listen Now

The future of Australian music lives in the underground — and we're down here with a microphone.

— Oz Soundz Unleashed Manifesto

The Format

What Makes
Us Different

  • 01
    Long-Form Interviews We don't do soundbites. Every conversation runs until there's nothing left to say. Real stories need room to breathe.
  • 02
    Grassroots & Established From bedroom artists to 30-year touring veterans — if you've got a real story, you've got a seat at the mic.
  • 03
    Australian Focus 100% dedicated to the Australian independent music scene. Local voices, local stories, local legend.
  • 04
    Industry Insight Booking agents, label owners, studio engineers — we pull back the curtain on the business behind the music.

The Voice
Behind The Mic

With over four decades of connection to the Australian music and audio industry at the grassroots level, the host of Oz Soundz Unleashed brings a depth of knowledge, passion, and genuine love for independent music that you simply can't fake.

From backyard gigs to recording studios, from street press to social media, this is someone who has seen every chapter of the Australian indie music story — and is still writing it.
